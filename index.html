What We Do

Three core research directions

Our work connects computational modelling, electrophysiology data analysis, and open science infrastructure for microneurography research.

FAIR Microneurography

We build metadata standards, open data pipelines, and infrastructure for FAIR sharing of microneurography data — including odML-based templates and integration with NFDI4Health Local Data Hubs.

Simulational Modelling of Peripheral Nerves

We implement biophysical models of unmyelinated C-fiber axons and DRG somas based on Hodgkin-Huxley equations, and develop machine-learning models to predict activity-dependent conduction velocity changes.

Microneurography Data Analysis

We develop and benchmark supervised spike sorting algorithms for noisy single-electrode recordings, using a marking-method ground-truth protocol, and build open frameworks for end-to-end MNG data analysis.
